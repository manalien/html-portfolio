The Best Movies According to Joyce

My top 4 movies of all-time.

12 Angry Men

This is probably my favorite movie...you just need to watch this

Taxi Driver

I can't really rank these movies but taxi driver is the one that makes me think the most. Sometimes I just find myself looking back at this film and i love it whole heartedly, even though I can't claim to really understand it.

Knives Out

I absolutely love mystery movies and this was the most recent one I watched and then I watched it two more times. I just love the story and the main character.

And Then There Were None(1945)

I've already stated my love for mysteries and I adore this film based on one of my favorite books with a more humorous tone. It's very charming and fun.
